CalCentral
Full Stack Developer

CalCentral is UC Berkeley's new online portal. It provides a unified and personalized one-stop way to access your UC Berkeley email, calendar, shared documents and class collaboration spaces, as well as links to key campus services and resources. It is where students can receive timely alerts about their registration status, blocks, and financial aid messages. Additionally, students can review their financial transactions, and see their class enrollments and class assignments.

CalCentral is an open source project. See my contributions to the repository here.
